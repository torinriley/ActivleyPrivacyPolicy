Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 25, 2025

At Activly, your privacy is a top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our iOS app.

1. Data Collection

Activly does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or health-related data to our servers or any third-party services. All data remains securely stored on your device.

We may request permission to access certain Apple HealthKit data to provide you with personalized health and fitness features. This access is optional and only used locally on your device.

2. HealthKit & Apple APIs

Activly integrates with Apple HealthKit to:

  • Read workout data
  • Display health metrics
  • Track activity trends
This information is never shared with anyone, and we do not use it for advertising or analytics.

We follow Apple’s HealthKit guidelines and only use this data to enhance your experience in the app.

3. No Third-Party Access

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking libraries, or ad networks. Your data never leaves your device.

4. Your Control

You can revoke any granted permissions at any time via your iOS device settings. If you delete the app, all data is erased from your device.
